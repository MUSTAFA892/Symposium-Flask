Box Cricket:

In the Box Cricket Challenge, participants engage in a dynamic, compact-format cricket tournament. Teams compete in fast-paced matches within a confined space, requiring quick reflexes and strategic play. Success is determined by team coordination, skillful batting, and innovative strategies.


Ramp Walk:

In the Ramp Walk Showcase, participants take the stage to model and present their fashion sense. Contestants navigate the runway, showcasing style and confidence while adhering to a given theme. Judging is based on poise, creativity, and presentation. Step into the spotlight and impress with your flair and elegance!


Spotify:

In the Spotify Song Challenge, players wear headphones and guess the song their teammate is singing. In a twist, they also identify segments of a song that are muted. This fun and interactive event tests musical knowledge and listening skills. Join us to see who hits the right notes and spots the silences!


Mathiyosi:

In the Mathiyosi Challenge, players have 10 seconds to respond with answers that are intentionally irrelevant to the questions asked. This quirky and fast-paced event tests quick thinking and creativity. Get ready for unexpected answers and plenty of laughs!


Push-up:

In the Push-Up Challenge, participants perform as many push-ups as possible within one minute. The maximum count achieved is recorded. This event tests endurance and strength, pushing you to your limits. Join us to showcase your physical prowess and see how many push-ups you can conquer!
