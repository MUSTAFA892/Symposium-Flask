Best Prompting Rules:


  • Team Size: 1-3 members.
  • Total Rounds: 3 rounds.
  • Prompt Requirements:
    • The prompt should describe the image effectively.
    • Prompts will be tested with an AI image generator to determine accuracy.
    • Prompt should not exceed more than 2 lines.
    • In case of a tie, the team with the more accurate prompt wins.
  • Round 1:
    • 3 images will be displayed.
    • Teams must provide prompts within 30 seconds for each image.
    • Teams with at least 2 correct prompts advance to Round 2.
  • Round 2:
    • 5 images will be displayed.
    • Each image prompt must be provided within 20 seconds.
    • Top teams with accurate prompts move on to Round 3.
  • Round 3:
    • This round involves buzzers; teams take turns answering prompts.
    • A total of 10 images will be shown. Teams can hit the buzzer to answer.
    • Teams have 10 seconds to provide a prompt after hitting the buzzer.
    • If the time is exceeded or the prompt is incorrect, the next team gets a chance.
    • The team with the most accurate and correct prompts wins.
